About

Industrial Engineering, M.Sc.
Business Analyst

In 2022 I moved from my home country Iceland to Copenhagen, Denmark where I attend the Industrial Engineering Master's program at the Technical University of Denmark.

I am currently working on my master’s thesis, which focuses on multi-criteria decision-making for the maritime industry, addressing future investments in the energy sector.

Alongside my studies I work as a Business Development Analyst for the company Sýn, where I identify and analyze potential ventures and investments.

My main hobbies and interests are sports and exercise, creativity and innovation—such as creating this website and playing the piano—and spending time with my family and friends.
