ABOUT

I am originally from Reykjavik, Iceland, where I graduated with a B.Sc. degree in Industrial Engineering from the University of Iceland. 

I started my professional career in Iceland where I worked part time along side my studies at Arion bank as a Service agent and later a specialist in Service Management. After graduating from UI, I worked as a Business Analyst in Business Development for Sýn hf., which I continued part time while taking my Masters degree in Denmark.
